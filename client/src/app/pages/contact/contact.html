Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PortHub

How do I get started with PortHub?

Simply sign up for a free account, choose a template, and start uploading your work. Our intuitive editor makes it easy to customize your portfolio.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! Pro and Premium plans include custom domain support, letting you showcase your portfolio at yourname.com.

Is there a mobile app?

While we don't have a dedicated app yet, PortHub works perfectly on all mobile devices through your browser.

What file types can I upload?

We support all major formats including images (JPG, PNG, GIF), videos (MP4, MOV), documents (PDF), and more.

Do you offer team accounts?

Yes! Our Team plan allows multiple users to collaborate on portfolios with shared assets and unified branding.

How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade security with SSL encryption, regular backups, and strict privacy controls to keep your work safe.
